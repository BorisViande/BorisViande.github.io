Lunar Integrated Shelter for Exploration (LISE) > Lunar Integrated Shelter for Exploration (LISE)

 This model is aimed at providing an overal understanding of the operational context, functional architecture, logical architecture and physical architecture of the Lunar Intergated Shelter for Exploration. It also includes considerations of operational constraints, system tied requirements and breakdown structures for system functions and elements. 
The System of Interest (SOI) is a deployable habitation module designed to host 2 astronauts for a minimum of 8 days on the moon. It is meant to be reusable with a reuse cycle of 28 days.
